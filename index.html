Hello! I am Mahendra

Thank you for visiting my portfolio. I am a digital product designer and cognitive science researcher with interests in UX Design / UX Reseach and Entrepreneurship. I have over a year of experience working with technology companies in UI/UX design roles.
I am currently pursuing MSc. Cognitive Science at University College Dublin and I graduated from IIT Guwahati with Bachelor of Design focusing in New Media and Interaction Design.

Why choose me?

I find designing digital products to be an enriching experience, I understand the iterative nature of design and I never shy away from creating multiple mockups even for a simple feature.
I am proficient in conducting user studies and creating intermediate deliverables such as site maps, information architecture, user journey maps and quick wireframes which makes the design process transparent and collaborative.

What I do?

I love working on early-stage products and building interfaces from scratch, using tools such as Sketch and Adobe XD.
Deliverables usually include a styleguide (Ex: iconography, color-guide etc.) using Zeplin, detailed documentation of the final design and an interactive prototype using Adobe XD or Invision.

My Specialties

I don't limit myself to strictly design tasks, I dedicate time to conduct design reviews once the product is developed and convey any mismatches to developers. My knowledge in web & app development helps me in collaborating with developers and tackling such problems. I give additional attention to micro-interactivity and make sure that the behaviour of UI is properly conveyed to developers by providing scrollable and clickable prototypes.
I have experience working on projects using design systems such as Bootstrap (for web), Angular/Material (for web), Material Design(Android UI), iOS UI and Semantic-UI. I create UI in adherence to such systems and I can even help front-end developers in this regard.
